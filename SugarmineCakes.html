Our Story

Born from Passion,
Baked with Love

At Sugarmine Cakes, every creation begins with a simple belief: that the most unforgettable moments in life deserve the most unforgettable cakes. Founded with a passion for the art of baking, we've grown into Ibadan's most trusted premium cake boutique.

We use only the finest quality ingredients — rich Belgian chocolate, fresh cream, premium vanilla, and hand-selected fruits — because we believe you deserve nothing less. Every cake is mixed, baked, and decorated by hand, ensuring a level of quality and care that no mass-produced bakery can match.

From intimate birthday celebrations to grand wedding receptions, from graduation parties to baby showers — we pour our hearts into every tier, every flower, every inscription. We don't just bake cakes; we craft memories.

Proudly serving the Ibadan community with same-day and scheduled deliveries, custom designs, and a commitment to making your sweet moments sweeter.
